I am a Master's student in Robotics, Systems and Control at ETH Zurich. My research interests lie on legged robots, with a focus on learning-based control and motion imitation. You can find more about my research on the [projects page](/projects/).

Born in Seoul, I have lived in Seoul, Singapore, Oxford and now Zurich. You can find more about my background on the [CV page](/cv/). I am always open to discussing research and collaboration opportunities, so feel free to reach out via email or social media.
